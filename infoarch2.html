Information Architecture II

Spring 2019

Interactive Narrative is a form of enterntainment in which the storyline is not predetermined. The author cretaes the setting, characters, and situation which the narrative must address, but the user (also reader or player) experiences a unique story based on their choices with the story world.

exaples include choose your own adventure books, interactive plays, Black Mirror: Bandersnatch, interactive DVD movies

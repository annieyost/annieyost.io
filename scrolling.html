Scrolling through Harvard Yard

To originally collect data, I went to Harvard yard and observed people while they walked through the yard. I found an overwhelming amount of phone use and multitasking. I decided to represent the data in two categories, phone-use and non-phone-use. I created symbols to represent different activities and color indicate gender and age.

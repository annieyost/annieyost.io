kevita kombucha brand redesign

In my Graphic Design Studio II class, we were able to choose a beverage brand that we wanted to redesign. After recently getting into kombucha, I decided to redesign one of the most popular brands out there, Kevita.

When applying the logo, I decided to use a band around a smaller glass bottle. The colors and pattern again embody the energizing and organic kombucha product itself.
